Medellín modern city culture skyline

Medellín is known as the City of Eternal Spring because of its pleasant weather all year round. The city offers a mix of culture, history, modern buildings, and friendly people. It has transformed into one of the most innovative cities in Latin America.

Keywords: Medellín, modern city, culture, skyline

Where to go

Keywords: Comuna 13, Plaza Botero, sculptures, graffiti

Comuna 13

Comuna 13

You must visit Comuna 13, a neighborhood full of colorful graffiti and powerful stories. You can walk around its streets and check out the street art, and you might meet locals who share their experiences. You should take photos and join a walking tour to learn more.

Plaza Botero

Plaza Botero

You should look around and admire the huge sculptures made by Fernando Botero. You could sit down in the square and enjoy the artistic vibe of the city. Don't forget to visit the Museum of Antioquia close by.

What to do

Keywords: cable cars, local food, nightlife, museums

  1. Ride the Metrocable: You should ride the Metrocable to enjoy the best views of the city. If you take the cable car on a sunny day, you will see the entire valley.
  2. Try local food: You must try bandeja paisa. If you eat it, you will understand why Medellín is famous for its cuisine.
  3. Enjoy the nightlife: You could go to El Poblado at night. If you visit on a weekend, you will find lively bars and clubs.
  4. Visit museums: You should visit the Museum of Antioquia. If you go inside, you might learn about Colombian history and Botero’s art.

Travel tips

Keywords: transportation, safety, weather, planning

  • You must use the Metro system because it is safe and efficient.
  • You should always carry an umbrella, as the weather can change suddenly.
  • You have to be careful with your belongings in crowded places.
  • You might want to book tours in advance to avoid long lines.
